Arts

The arts comprise of visual art, dance, music, literary and performing, all of which women have contributed to throughout history. Barriers to success and acceptance in many of these spaces is still with us today, including stark statistic that within galleries in London, 78% represent more men than women, with just 5% having equal representation. Often these barriers inform their creative practices, at the Museum of Female Achievement we celebrate the creative expressions women throughout history have brought to the world.
